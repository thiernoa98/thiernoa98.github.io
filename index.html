About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Thierno, and I am a software developer from Guinea. In 2014, I traveled over 5,000 miles to seek better education and greater opportunities. Coming from a French speaking country, I came to the US with absolute zero knowledge of English. However, coming here with a goal and a strong desire to learn, I managed to complete four years of high school and graduate from college with a Bachelor’s degree in Computer Information System/programming. Now I can speak French and English, and I can write in multiple programming languages such as JavaScript, NodeJs, React HTML5, JAVA, PHP, CSS3, SQL and more! I'm learning new things and face new challenges every day, and there is no limit to where I will go next.
